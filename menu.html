🍚 Rice Dishes

Our rice dishes are the heart of Lagos Kitchen. From the smoky and flavorful Jellof Rice to the colorful and fragrant Fried Rice, each plate is a celebration of Nigerian culinary tradition. Cooked with the finest long-grain rice, fresh tomatoes, peppers, and a blend of secret spices passed down through generations. Prices start from just ¥42!

🍲 Traditional Soups

Experience the soul of Nigerian cuisine with our traditional soups. Egusi Soup is a rich melon seed delicacy, Banga Soup brings the nutty flavor of palm kernel, and Okra Soup offers a slimy, nutritious delight. Each soup is slow-cooked to perfection and best paired with your choice of swallow. Prices range from ¥50 to ¥55.

🥐 Snacks & Bites

Perfect for a quick bite or street food craving! Our Puff Puff are sweet fried dough balls, Egg Rolls are crispy pastries with a whole egg inside, and Sharwama is our famous grilled chicken wrap. Don't forget our soft Chapati flatbread! Prices from just ¥10 to ¥35.

🥤 Drinks

Quench your thirst with our refreshing Zobo drink! Made from hibiscus flowers, ginger, and pineapple, this vibrant red beverage is sweet, tangy, and packed with health benefits. The perfect companion to any spicy Nigerian meal. Only ¥12 per cup!
